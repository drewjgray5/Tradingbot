# Short-Form Marketing Video Package

## 1) Core Angle

**Positioning angle:**  
Your bot is a **trading operating system** that helps people move from manual, emotional execution to a repeatable workflow with guardrails.

**Single promise:**  
**Trade faster with guardrails, not guesswork.**

**Audience:**  
Active retail swing traders using Discord and broker APIs who feel friction between finding setups and executing safely.

**Compliance-safe framing:**  
Decision support and execution workflow tooling. No guaranteed returns. Not financial advice.

---

## 2) Hooks + Final Script (45-60s)

## Hook Variants (first 2-3 seconds)

1. **"Most traders don't lose because of bad ideas. They lose in bad execution."**  
2. **"You don't need more signals. You need a system that stops dumb trades."**  
3. **"What if your trade setup, risk checks, and execution all happened in one flow?"**

## Final Script (60s)

**0:00-0:03 (Hook)**  
"Most traders don't lose because of bad ideas. They lose in bad execution."

**0:03-0:10 (Problem)**  
"You scan charts, second-guess entries, and by the time you act, the move is gone or risk is wrong."

**0:10-0:25 (Solution: workflow)**  
"This bot runs your setup flow end-to-end: scans for high-quality breakouts, scores them, checks trend and sector strength, then pushes actionable alerts straight to Discord."

**0:25-0:38 (Guardrails + confidence)**  
"Before execution, it applies guardrails for position limits and daily trade limits, and requires explicit confirmation. So you move faster without giving up control."

**0:38-0:48 (Credibility)**  
"It logs outcomes, tracks what worked, and improves filtering over time. You get cleaner signals and a process you can actually trust."

**0:48-0:60 (CTA + disclaimer)**  
"If you want disciplined execution without the chaos, follow and join the demo waitlist.  
For education only, not financial advice."

## 4) Editing Kit

## Voiceover Copy
Use the final 60s script above as VO. Read pace target: ~140-150 words/min.

## On-Screen Text Rules
- Max 6-8 words per line
- Keep key terms: `Scan`, `Score`, `Guardrails`, `Confirm`, `Execute`
- Use strong contrast and center-safe placement for vertical 9:16

## B-Roll/Screen Capture Checklist
- Scanner result screen (symbol + score)
- Discord signal card + confirmation buttons
- Guardrail block/allow examples
- Weekly digest and signal quality summaries
- One clean broker-side “order placed” confirmation (sensitive info blurred)

## Sound Design Notes
- Intro impact hit at 0:00
- Soft riser under problem section
- Subtle click sounds on checklist/confirm moments
- Clean bass drop at CTA

## CapCut/Premiere Notes
- Sequence: 1080x1920, 30fps
- Use jump cuts every 1.5-2.5s to retain attention
- Burned-in captions with keyword highlights in brand color
- Keep safe margins for TikTok/Reels UI
